Competitive Moat
9
Canva Ecosystem Lock-In
Restaurant owners already live in Canva. Servvee is the missing bridge from Canva to their website. Switching requires learning a new design tool — most restaurants won't.
8
Holiday Scheduling Logic
The `resolve_active_promo()` scheduling engine is a defensible product feature. Set-and-forget seasonal promos become a sticky habit that competitors can't easily replicate without the same UX.
7
Platform-Agnostic Embed
One `<iframe>` works on any website — WordPress, Squarespace, Wix, Webflow, custom HTML. Zero dependency on a specific CMS. Restaurants don't have to change anything they've built.
6
Agency Channel Multiplier
Each Agency account represents 15–30 restaurant clients. Winning 100 agencies = 1,500–3,000 restaurants without direct customer acquisition cost. Agencies become a sales force.
Growth Levers by Phase
Year 1
SEO + Paid Search
Target "restaurant menu update tool", "Canva restaurant website", "automatic menu website update". Low competition, high intent. $500/mo Google Ads budget with SEO blog content.
Est. 15–25 signups/mo
Year 1
Canva Community + Templates
Publish free restaurant menu templates on Canva template marketplace with "Powered by Servvee" watermark in the description. Template traffic converts at high intent.
Est. 8–12 signups/mo organic
Year 1
Referral Program
20% first-month commission for restaurant owners who refer other restaurant owners. Target local restaurant associations, Facebook groups, Chamber of Commerce networks.
Est. 5–10 signups/mo
Year 2
Canva App Marketplace
Submit Servvee as a Canva App. Restaurant owners discover it while designing their menu — one-click connect sends the design to their website. The lowest-friction possible onboarding.
Est. 50–100 signups/mo at scale
Year 2
WordPress Plugin
A lightweight WP plugin that auto-embeds the Servvee iframe by user ID + slot. Listed on WordPress.org plugin directory. 10M+ restaurant WordPress sites as addressable market.
Est. 30–60 signups/mo
Year 2
Restaurant Association Partnerships
Partner with state restaurant associations (NRA affiliates) to offer discounted Starter plans to members. Associations provide implicit endorsement and direct email list access.
Est. 40–80 signups/mo per partner
Year 3
POS Integration (Toast, Square)
Bi-directional sync: price changes in POS auto-update the Canva menu template via the Make.com automation. Creates near-unbreakable retention for multi-location Pro/Agency clients.
2× retention improvement
Year 3
White-Label Reseller Program
Digital agencies buy Agency seats at $199 and resell to clients at $79–149 with their own branding. 40% margin on resale makes it attractive. Target web design agencies serving restaurants.
Each reseller = 5–20 restaurants
Year 3
AI Menu Optimization
Claude-powered: analyze menu photos, suggest seasonal specials, auto-generate holiday promo copy, predict high-traffic dates. Premium feature unlocks at Pro+, justifies price increase.
$10–20/mo upsell per user
